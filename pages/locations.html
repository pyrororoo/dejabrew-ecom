Locations

Deja Brew is proud to serve the beautiful regions of Bicol and Albay. With several locations, we’re never far from where you are. Whether you’re enjoying the stunning views of Mayon Volcano or exploring the lively streets of Legazpi, you can find us ready to serve you our signature blends and homemade treats. Read more for specific addresses and operating hours to find the closest Deja Brew to you!

Deja Brew Legazpi

123 Rizal Street, Legazpi City, Albay
Monday to Friday: 7:00 AM - 9:00 PM
Saturday & Sunday: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM

With its stylish yet cozy interior, Deja Brew’s Legazpi branch is a favorite among students, professionals, and travelers looking for a calm environment to work or relax. Free WiFi and comfortable seating make it ideal for both focused work sessions and leisurely coffee breaks. Open from 6:30 am to 8:30 pm on weekdays and extending to 10:30 pm on weekends, it’s the perfect spot to start or end the day.

Deja Brew Daraga

456 Mayon Avenue, Daraga, Albay
Monday to Friday: 6:30 AM - 8:30 PM
Saturday & Sunday: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM

In the heart of historic Daraga, this Deja Brew branch provides a serene setting with a thoughtfully designed aesthetic that feels intimate and welcoming. The calm atmosphere and dependable free WiFi make it a preferred workspace for students and employees, while weekend hours from 8:00 am to 10:30 pm allow for extended visits. It’s a prime location for those looking to enjoy a productive yet peaceful coffee experience.

Deja Brew Tabaco

789 Pio Duran Road, Tabaco City, Albay
Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
Saturday: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
Sunday: Closed

Deja Brew’s Tabaco branch has a modern aesthetic and relaxed vibe, creating an inviting spot for people on the go. With a good working space and free WiFi, it’s a convenient stopover for students and casuals alike. Open on weekdays from 6:30 am to 8:30 pm and Saturdays from 8:00 am to 10:30 pm, the Tabaco branch is closed on Sundays, making it a popular destination during the week.
